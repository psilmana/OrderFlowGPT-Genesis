# Release Notes — 0.1.14

## Milestone 20 — Deterministic Delta Analysis

- Added immutable deterministic delta models and `FootprintDeltaAnalyzer`.
- Added cell, row, footprint, and statistics aggregation over immutable `FootprintMatrix` data.
- Integrated delta analysis into `SequentialObjectDetectionPipeline` immediately after Absorption Detection and exposed results through `DetectionGraph` helpers.
- Delta Analysis computes delta values only: no divergence, trend analysis, market prediction, market bias, or trading signals.
- Milestone 21 introduces Volume Cluster Analysis.
