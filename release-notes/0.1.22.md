# Release 0.1.22 - Bundle 8 Multi-Timeframe Context

Bundle 8 implements the Genesis Multi-Timeframe Context Engine.

## Added

- Timeframe models and analyzer for Tick, 1 Minute, 5 Minute, 15 Minute, 30 Minute, 1 Hour, 4 Hour, Daily, and Unknown contexts.
- Multi-timeframe alignment with Fully Aligned, Partially Aligned, Opposing, and Neutral classifications.
- Context aggregation over immutable outputs from previous Genesis bundles.
- Confluence engine with Strong, Moderate, Weak, and No Confluence classifications.
- DetectionGraph fields, lookup helpers, and statistics helpers for timeframe context, alignment, context aggregation, and confluence.
- Sequential pipeline integration after Session Intelligence.

## Safety and scope

Genesis remains deterministic and does not perform AI reasoning, prediction, trade recommendations, probability estimation, machine learning, strategy generation, or trade signal generation.

Bundle 9 introduces the Dataset Builder for Fabio video learning.
