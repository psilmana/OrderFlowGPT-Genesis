# Release Notes - 0.1.19

Bundle 4 introduces deterministic Advanced Order Flow Analytics.

## Added

- Delta Divergence Detection models, configuration, statistics, result, and analyzer.
- Cumulative Delta models, configuration, statistics, result, and analyzer.
- Delta Momentum models, configuration, statistics, result, and analyzer.
- Exhaustion Detection models, configuration, statistics, result, and detector.
- DetectionGraph lookup/statistics helpers and SequentialObjectDetectionPipeline integration after Auction Market Theory.

## Scope

Genesis still does not perform AI reasoning, prediction, trading signals, probability estimation, machine learning, OCR modifications, OpenCV additions, networking, threading, async processing, randomness, trade inference, recommendations, or vendor-specific logic. Bundle 5 introduces Market Structure Analysis.
