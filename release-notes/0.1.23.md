# 0.1.23 - Bundle 12 Fabio Knowledge Extraction

Bundle 12 creates deterministic Fabio teaching datasets by combining synchronized Fabio videos, transcript alignment, timeline/frame references, and Genesis DetectionGraph references.

This release adds immutable knowledge identifiers, metadata, categories, topics, statements, contexts, observations, references, timelines, datasets, statistics, configuration, results, and extraction engine APIs.

Bundle 12 explicitly includes NO learning, NO prediction, NO AI reasoning, NO strategy generation, NO trade recommendations, NO probabilities, NO OCR changes, and NO OpenCV changes. Fabio is the only knowledge source.

Bundle 13 introduces the Learning Engine.
