# Release Notes - 0.1.9 Milestone 15

Milestone 15 introduces Footprint Semantic Interpretation. Genesis can now assign market meaning to validated numeric footprint values from classified cell regions, including bid volume, ask volume, delta, total volume, empty cells, unknown cells, and invalid mappings.

## Added

- Immutable semantic interpretation models for footprint values, interpreted cells, grid-level interpretations, interpretation warnings, and result wrappers.
- `FootprintSemanticType` with `UNKNOWN`, `BID_VOLUME`, `ASK_VOLUME`, `DELTA`, `TOTAL_VOLUME`, `EMPTY`, and `INVALID`.
- `SemanticMapper`, `FootprintInterpreter`, and `InterpretationPipeline` contracts.
- Deterministic default implementations that depend only on `CellClassification` and validated parsed values.
- `DetectionGraph` integration for footprint cells, optional footprint interpretation, and lookup helpers for cell, bid, ask, delta, and total-volume values.

## Still out of scope

Genesis still does not detect imbalances, absorption, stacked imbalances, unfinished auctions, calculate signals, make trading decisions, or generate trades. Milestone 16 introduces Imbalance Detection.
